Good to know when renting in Cyprus

Car rental under 25
in Cyprus

You must be at least 21 years old with
a license held for 3 years to rent a car
from SIXT. An underage driver fee applies.
Some car categories require the driver to be 25.

Driver's License Requirements

Your license must have been valid for 3
years. If it is in a non-Roman alphabet
it must be accompanied by an International
Driving Permit. Chinese drivers need a
translation of their license

One-way car rental in
Cyprus

One-way rentals between SIXT stations
are allowed for some vehicles. An
additional fee may apply.
